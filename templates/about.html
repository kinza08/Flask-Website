About Us


CrossFit Gym was started by Alex and Sparky in 2012. It’s our mission to help people in Denver get fit, stay healthy, and live life on their terms without fitness being an obstacle,A gym session or brisk walk can help. Physical activity stimulates many brain chemicals that may leave you feeling happier, more relaxed and less anxious. You also may feel better about your appearance and yourself when you exercise regularly, which can boost your confidence and improve your self-esteem.
